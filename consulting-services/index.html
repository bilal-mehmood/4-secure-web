Consulting Services

  • 4 Secure provides network consulting to the local and international enterprises.
  • We believe that what differentiates a good consultancy from traditional consultancy is by having passion and drive for excellence. It’s a combination of knowledge, experience and good judgment.
  • Generally, Networks are built to enable business and we make sure that we understand that business first. We fix the cause and not just the symptoms.
  • We have been able to retain a successful converged network consultancy division, specializing in IP telephony, wireless solutions, network security and remote access.
  • Our pool of consultants and highly skilled engineers have many years’ experiences in the IT field and hold numerous certifications from major partners such as Cisco, McAfee, Avaya, 3M, IBM and Juniper.
  • Our consultants and engineers are available for timed contracts, specific projects or on a daily basis.
  • A policy of both in-house and off-site training is employed to ensure that ABM staff are up to date on new equipment and emerging technologies.
  • Services that we offer in Consulting include analysis of specific projects and problems; documentation of options with analysis of relative strengths and weaknesses.
